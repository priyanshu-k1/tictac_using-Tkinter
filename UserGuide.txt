HOW TO PLAY:

*To rename the "player 1" and "player 2":
    
    >Simply click on "player 1" or "player 2".
    >A window will appear asking for players names.
    >After enterning the names of the players click on submit button.

*To restart the game:
    >Click on "New game" button in the main window.

*To enable A.I mode:
    >Click on "Against A.I" button in the main window.

*To disable A.I mode:
    >Click on "Against Human" button in the main window.

*To Mute the game:
    >Click on "Mute" button in the main window.

*TO unmute the game:
    >Click on "Unmute" button in the main window.
     
Hope this helps.
